About Hackverse 1.0

Hackverse 1.0 is the flagship hackathon of Raghu Engineering College, conducted in collaboration with GDG on Campus REC and E-Cell REC. It is a platform where innovation meets execution, bringing together students, creators, and problem-solvers to build impactful solutions that address real-world challenges.This hackathon is designed to spark creativity, encourage collaboration, and showcase the power of technology-driven ideas. Our approach is simple, smart, and scalable—just like the solutions we aim to create.

Hackthon

A hackathon is an intense, sprint-like event where tech enthusiasts collaborate to build a software or hardware project from scratch. Typically lasting a weekend, participants race against the clock to turn innovative ideas into functional prototypes.

Campfire

As evening approaches, we'll gather together around a crackling campfire. The night will be brought to life with a mini music concert featuring live acoustic performances. Get ready for a memorable evening of warmth, melodies, and great company under the stars.

Hybrid Form

Our hackathon is being hosted in a tailored hybrid format to accommodate innovators across India. To foster a strong local community, on-site participation is required for local attendees. The online mode is exclusively available for participants joining us from any other state.

Personal Feedback

For all teams that do not advance to the final round, we have arranged a valuable next step. Each eliminated team is guaranteed a personal one-on-one session with members of our judging panel. This is a unique opportunity to receive direct, constructive feedback to help refine and improve your project.
